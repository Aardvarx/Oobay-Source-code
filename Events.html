Oobay 2.21 is on the horizon!

This update is jam-packed with tons of brand new features!

Digital Items! (No, not quite NFTs)

Introducing the Digital Items category, allowing users to buy and sell digital goods such as e-books, software licenses, and digital art!

Sellers can now opt for digital delivery, delivering items directly to buyers via email for instant access.

Branded Items!

You can now specify the brand of an item to sell preowned items under renowned brand names or new items offered by established companies!

Explore a curated selection of quality products from trusted brands within the Oobay marketplace.

(OOBAY IS NOT ENDORSED BY NOR ASSOCIATED WITH SELECTABLE BRANDS)

Oobay Ambassador program returns!

The Oobay Ambassador Program is back, offering dedicated users exclusive rewards for promoting the platform.

Earn bonuses by recruiting new members to join the Oobay Community!

Check out new categories!

Real Estate

Explore listings for properties, land, and real estate services in the newly added Real Estate category.

Buy, sell, or rent residential and commercial properties directly through Oobay!

Automobiles and Auto Parts

Browse a wide selection of vehicles, auto parts, and automotive services in the Automobiles and Auto Parts category.

Find your dream car or essential spare parts conveniently within the Oobay marketplace!

Tickets

Discover tickets for events, concerts, shows, and more in the Tickets category.

Purchase tickets for your favorite events directly through Oobay's secure platform!

Music

Dive into the world of music with the new Music category, offering a diverse range of albums, singles, and musical instruments.

Buy, sell, or trade music-related items with fellow enthusiasts on Oobay!

Additional Features:

Scrolling tips on Homescreen

Receive helpful tips on selling and buying while navigating the Oobay platform through the new scrolling tips feature on the homescreen!

Name Change

Change your username for 500,000 Oob, allowing you to personalize your identity within the Oobay community!

And last but not least...

Ranks are coming to Oobay!

Ranks help really distinguish the Oobay pros from the noobs. Check out the ranks page on MyOobay for more on how ranks will work!

Go to Ranks page

Release date

Oobay version 2.21 is expected to release by the end of June, but possibly later.

Use code "v2.21" for 5,000 Oobay Points!
